Dominican Republic

Food:

Dominican Republic is known for its variety of delicious foods.Some of Dominican Republic'c main dishes are mungu, which is smashed plantain, with salami,eggs, fried cheese, onions, and avocado. There's also chimichuri, which is a hamburger with cabbage,and tomatoes on italian bread. There's also sancocho, which is a soup made of beef, chicken, corn, plantains, potatoe, and can be eaten with a bowl of rice and a piece of avoacado.


Music:

The Dominican Republic has a diversity of different type songs like merengue, batchata, and dembow. Merengue is is a style of Dominican music and dance. Partners hold each other in a closed position. The leader holds the follower's waist with the leader's right hand, while holding the follower's right hand with the leader's left hand at the follower's eye level. Bachata is a style of dance that originated in the Dominican Republic. It is danced widely all over the world but not identically. The basics to the dance are three-step with a Cuban hip motion, followed by a tap including a hip movement on the 4th beat. The main element of dembow music is its rhythm, which is somewhat reminiscent of reggaeton and dancehall music, but with a more constant rhythm and faster than reggaeton.
