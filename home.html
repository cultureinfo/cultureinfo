Welcome to CULTURE+!

This site aims to provide information about different cultures to help people better understand people of different cultures.
Different cultures have very different customs and traditions. It is important to be considerate of other people's cultures, both in an out of the workplace. Our goal is to make people more aware of different cultures.

A fish only discovers its need for water when it is no longer in it. Our culture is like water to a fish. It sustains us. Our own culture is like water to a fish. We live and breathe through it - Fiona Trompeneaars + Charles Hampton Turner

Cultural awareness is the foundation of communication. People interpret events in different ways. What is considered appropriate behavior in one culture is frequently inappropriate in another. Misunderstandings arise when people use their own meanings to define another's reality.

Becoming aware of our own cultural dynamics is not easy and guessed similarities could lead to misinterpretation.

Celebrate diversity.
